Cookies

Description

Cookies are sweet, baked treats that come in many flavors, often made with ingredients like flour, sugar, butter, and chocolate chips. They're crisp on the outside, soft or chewy inside, and perfect for snacking or dessert.

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Egg
  • Baking Soda
  • Vannila Extract
  • Chocolate Chips
  1. Mix dry ingredients
  2. Cream butter sugar
  3. Add egg vanilla
  4. Combine all ingredients
  5. Fold in chips (optional)
  6. Scoop onto sheet
  7. Bake until golden
  8. Cool and enjoy
