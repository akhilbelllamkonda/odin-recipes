Microwave Mug Cakes

Decription

This microwave chocolate mug cake is a quick, single-serving dessert made with just a few pantry ingredients. It’s soft, rich, and ready in under two minutes—perfect for when you’re craving something sweet fast!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Cocoa
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Oil
  1. Mix dry ingredients in a mug.
  2. Add milk and oil, stir well.
  3. Optional: Add chips or flavoring.
  4. Microwave for 1 minute
